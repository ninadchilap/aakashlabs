Aakash Tablet is an Android-based tablet computers promoted by Government of India as part of an initiative to link 25,000 colleges and 400 universities in an e-learning program. Aakash tablet is part of the central government Rs 4612 crore national mission on education through information and communication technology (NMEICT), launched in 2009.
The main objective of the project at IIT Bombay, is empowerment of teachers and students in engineering colleges. We have already deployment Aakash tablets in a large number of engineering colleges in the country, with two objectives. The first is to test and enhance the effectiveness of these tablets for use in a classroom environment. The second is the development of new educational applications and contents on Aakash, largely through final year R&D projects done by BE/ME students. Read more...
